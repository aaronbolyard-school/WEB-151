The game has two means of monetization: cosmetics (weapons and outfits [skins] for Jason) and additional episodes.

The additional content is rather affordable:

  1. $1.99 for a skin and an episode (collection of levels). For example, buying Mama's Megamart unlocks Zombie Jason.
  2. $9.99 for all episodes and skins.

There's a "Pay To Unlock" button. Image courtesy of Marshall Walker.

Most content can be unlocked during gameplay.

During a 15 minute gameplay session (completing Episode 1, Crystal Lake Memories), the only time in-app purchase notifications appear is when viewing content not yet unlocked or hidden behind a purchase. There was also a mention when Jason went up a rank, which occurs every 4-5 levels.

The monetization strategy is well implemented and unobtrusive for the player, gaining notable mentions from sites like Eurogamer.
